Terms & policies

The fine print, in plain English.

Quotes & pricing

Delivered prices are estimates based on the square footage, depth and color you provide. Final pricing is confirmed in writing before delivery. Prices can change with material and freight costs until your order is confirmed.

Delivery

Supersacks are delivered on a flatbed truck on a pallet to your site. You're responsible for unloading with bucket forks, a forklift or rented equipment, and for safe truck access to the drop point.

Payment terms

Schools and municipalities qualify for Net 30 on request. All other orders are due on delivery unless arranged in advance.

Product

Playground material is IPEMA-certified and fall-height rated; documentation is available for inspections. Black is uncoated recycled rubber. Brown and red carry a 12-year color warranty.

Playground safety disclaimer

While 405 Rubber Mulch chunk nuggets are tested to meet standard ASTM F1292 impact-attenuation guidelines (providing up to a 10-foot critical fall height protection at a consolidated 6-inch depth), playground fall height requirements and mandatory safety depths can vary strictly by state, municipal code, and individual childcare licensing agencies. Final compliance and safety verification remain the sole responsibility of the buyer, the equipment manufacturer's specified fall height guidelines, and the local certified playground safety inspector (CPSI).
